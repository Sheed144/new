Demon Copperhead


Barbara Kingslover

Set in the mountains of southern Appalachia, Demon Copperhead is the story of a boy born to a teenaged single mother in a single-wide trailer,
with no assets beyond his dead father's good looks and copper-colored hair, a caustic wit, and a fierce talent for survival.


Desert Star


Micheal Connelly

LAPD detective Renée Ballard and Harry Bosch work together to hunt the killer
is Bosch's “white whale” a man responsible for the murder of an entire family.
A year has passed since LAPD detective Renée Ballard quit the force in the face of misogyny, demoralization, and endless red tape.


Triple Cross


James Patterson

James Patterson's #1 bestselling hero Detective Alex Cross hunts down a serial killer
who targets entire families and who will next be coming for the Crosses. A precise killer,
he always moves under the cover of darkness, flawlessly triggering no alarms, leaving no physical evidence.


Going Rogue


Janet Evanovich

When a special coin that in itself has no value, turns out to be worth more than believed,
Connie the office manager is kidnapped. Stephanie and Lula rush to find the missing coin.
One thing after another happens and events keep putting her in danger.


Mad Honey


Jodi Picoult & Jennifer Boylan

The shocking murder of a teenager thrusts a small town into the headlines and destabilizes the lives of everyone who knew her.
Olivia McAfee, a professional beekeeper and single mother, fled Boston and an abusive husband to try to give her son, Asher,
a better life in small-town New Hampshire.
